Jacobian of Elbow Manipulator

Definition of Residual

Convention of SE3 Distance

\[ \Delta T T_1 = T_2 \] \[ \Delta T = T_2 T_1 ^{-1} \]

Cost function

\[ {\arg} \min_{T_a} \| \ln (T_a T_d ^{-1})^ \vee \| \]

Where \(T_a\) is the actual pose of end tool, \(T_d\) is the desire pose of end tool.

For the one of 6 joints \(J_i\) , denote its pose \(T\)

\[ T_a = T_b T T_a \]

Where \(T_b\) is the pose of all the joints below \(J_i\), \(T_a\) is the pose of all the joints above \(J_i\).

Residual

\[ f(T) = \ln (T_b T T_a T_d ^{-1}) \]

Denote \(T_1 = T_b, T_2 = T_a T_d ^{-1}\) :

\[ f(T) = \ln (T_1 T T_2)^ \vee \]

2. Jacobian

\[ \begin{align} \frac{df(T)}{dT} &= \frac{d}{dT} \ln (T_1 T T_2) \tag{2.1} \\ &= \lim_{\xi \to 0} \frac{ \ln (T_1 \exp (\xi ^ \wedge ) T T_2)^ \vee - \ln (T_1 T T_2)^ \vee}{\xi} \\ &= \lim_{\xi \to 0} \frac{ \ln (T_1 \exp (\xi ^ \wedge ) (T_1 ^{-1} T_1) T T_2)^ \vee - \ln (T_1 T T_2)^ \vee}{\xi} \tag{2.3}\\ &= \lim_{\xi \to 0} \frac{ \ln ( \exp( (Ad(T_1) \xi)^ \wedge ) T_1 T T_2 )^ \vee - \ln (T_1 T T_2)^ \vee}{\xi} \tag{2.4}\\ &= \lim_{\xi \to 0} \frac{ \mathcal{J} ^{-1} ( \ln (T_1 T T_2) (Ad(T_1) \xi) + \ln (T_1 T T_2)^ \vee - \ln (T_1 T T_2)^ \vee ) }{\xi} \tag{2.5}\\ &= \lim_{\xi \to 0} \frac{ \mathcal{J} ^{-1} ( \ln (T_1 T T_2)) (Ad(T_1) \xi) }{\xi} \\ &= \mathcal{J} ^{-1} ( \ln (T_1 T T_2)) Ad(T_1) \end{align} \]

For Elbow Manipulator, a joint with axis output, only rotate around the Z-axis. i.e. \(T = T_z T_{\theta}\) , where \(T_z\) is the zero position of the joint, \(T_{\theta}\) is the transform from motor rotation. For a joint with its axis as input, \(T = T_{\theta} ^{-1} T_z ^{-1} \).

Summary

For axis output joint \(J_i\)

\[ \frac{df(T_{\theta})}{d T_{\theta}} = \mathcal{J} ^{-1} ( \ln (T_1 T_z T_{\theta} T_2)) Ad(T_1) \]

For axis input joint \(J_i\)

\[ \frac{df(T_{\theta})}{d T_{\theta}} = \mathcal{J} ^{-1} ( \ln (T_1 T_{\theta} ^{-1} T_z ^{-1} T_2)) Ad(T_1) \]
